Frequently Asked Questions

What can I expect from my first appointment?

The assessment is always our first step and arguably one of the most important parts of the whole process for you to regain a pain-free life and continue enjoying your favourite sports and activities. We will talk through your symptoms and past medical history before completing a thorough hands-on assessment to examine aspects like your range of motion, strength and functional biomechanics.

The knowledge we have gained from our degrees and post graduate qualifications form the scientific foundations but there is much more involved in the treatment and rehabilitation process so we will always ensure you are involved with the decision making once a working diagnosis has been made.

No two injuries are the same and your life commitments, time restrictions, and motivations play as big a part as anything! There is much you can do beyond your appointments, and this is an area we are eager to address to prevent your symptoms from recurring after receiving a temporary quick fix.

What should I wear to my appointment?

To allow for a thorough assessment, we will ask to see the area in question and so this may require you to remove outer clothing. If you feel more comfortable in shorts and a vest top, for example, please bring these along. We have changing facilities on site. If you are a runner, it is often useful for us to see your trainers, too.

Do I need to bring anything with me?

You do not need to bring anything in particular with you unless you have had previous investigations and in which case, a copy of any scan results or referral letters will be great to see.

Can I claim the sessions through my insurance?

All insurance companies work differently so please contact us on an individual basis but yes, there are some that you can claim through. Most of our work is self-funded but please enquire for more information.

Do you treat clients who are younger than 18 years old?

We treat clients from age 12. We request that clients under the age of 18 are accompanied by their parent or guardian when attending the clinic.

How do I make a booking?

Our online booking system is the easiest way to view our therapists' schedules and to book an appointment.

Is there parking on site?

Ample parking is available free of charge on site.
