About Us

The Rehab Lab is brought to you by Father and Son duo Jeremy and Jacob Ogle.

Both sportsmen are fully trained, qualified, and insured Sports Therapists, and members of the Society of Sports Therapists.

Jeremy studied at Rupert Stanley College and has been providing Sports Therapy Services in Dromore since 2014.

Jacob studied at Hartpury University, achieving a First Class Honours degree.
